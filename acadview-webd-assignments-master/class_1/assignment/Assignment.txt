Steps 1: use <nav> </nav> for navigation
e.g 
<nav>
	<ul>
		<li>link 1</li>
		<li>link 2</li>
		..
		..
		..
		<li>link n</li>
	</ul>
</nav>

Steps 2: use heading tag for different titles of recipes
e.g <h2>Soup</h2>

use <img src="image path"> for images

and finally use ordered list or unordered list for recipie steps and ingridients

<ol>
	<li></li>
</ol>
